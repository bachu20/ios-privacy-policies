Privacy Policy for Daily Dose

Last updated: August 5, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Dose ("we", "our", or "us") operates with complete privacy - we do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers. Everything you do with our mobile application ("Service") happens locally on your device only.

Daily Dose operates with zero data collection. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Store your data in the cloud
  • Share your information with third parties

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Personal Information (Device-Only)

  • Custom Affirmations: Text affirmations you create and save within the app (stored locally only)
  • Custom Themes: Personal affirmation categories you create with custom colors and icons (stored locally only)
  • App Settings: Your notification preferences, theme settings, and app configuration (stored locally only)
  • Usage Data: Analytics data for premium users (consistency tracking, usage patterns - stored locally only)
  • Voice Recordings: Optional voice recordings of your affirmations (stored locally only)

Device Information (Local Only)

  • Device Type: iOS device information for app optimization (processed locally only)
  • App Version: Current app version for support purposes (stored locally only)
  • Notification Settings: Your notification preferences and scheduling (stored locally only)

How Your Information is Used (Locally Only)

Primary Functions (All On-Device)

  • Daily Affirmations: Provide personalized daily affirmations based on your local usage patterns
  • Custom Content: Save and manage your personal affirmations and custom themes locally
  • AI Generation: Generate personalized affirmations based on your mood and goals using on-device AI (premium feature)
  • Notifications: Send scheduled reminders using local notifications (if enabled)
  • Analytics: Provide insights about your affirmation practice using local data only (premium feature)
  • Voice Recording: Record and transcribe your affirmations using local processing (optional feature)

Data Processing (100% Local)

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally on your device - never in the cloud
  • Zero External Transmission: We do not transmit any data to external servers
  • Complete Privacy: Your affirmations and settings remain completely private and local
  • On-Device Processing: All AI and analytics processing happens on your device
  • No Internet Required: The app works entirely offline after initial download

Data Storage and Security (100% Local)

Local Storage Only

  • All affirmations, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • Data is encrypted using iOS system encryption
  • No data is ever transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage or backup services are used

Data Retention (Device-Only)

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • We do not maintain any copies of your data anywhere
  • No external backups or syncing services

Third-Party Services (Minimal & Local)

Apple Services (Local Processing Only)

  • StoreKit: Used for in-app purchases and subscription management (no data collection)
  • UserNotifications: Used for local notification delivery (no external transmission)
  • Speech Recognition: Used for voice-to-text transcription (processed locally only)
  • Core ML: Used for on-device AI affirmation generation (premium feature - no external processing)
  • Natural Language: Used for text processing in AI generation (local processing only)

Zero Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, or similar services
  • No tracking cookies or advertising networks
  • No user behavior tracking or profiling
  • No crash reporting services that collect user data
  • No marketing or advertising services

Your Rights and Choices (Complete Control)

Data Control (100% Local)

  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to remove all stored data
  • Export Data: Currently not available, but planned for future updates
  • Modify Settings: Change notification preferences and app settings anytime
  • Complete Ownership: All your data belongs to you and stays on your device

Privacy Controls (No External Dependencies)

  • Notifications: Enable/disable notifications in app settings (local only)
  • Analytics: Premium analytics are optional and process data locally only
  • Voice Recording: Completely optional feature (local processing only)
  • AI Generation: Premium feature that processes data locally on your device
  • Custom Themes: Personal data that stays on your device only

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of collecting data from children under 13. All app usage is local to the device only.

International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please be aware that your information is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate external servers or databases, so your data remains in your local jurisdiction and is never transmitted across borders.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Since we do not collect, process, or store any personal information on external servers, GDPR compliance is simplified:

  • No Data Processing: We do not process personal data on external servers
  • Local Processing Only: All data processing happens locally on your device
  • No Data Transfers: No data is transferred outside your device
  • User Control: You have complete control over your local data

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Since we do not collect, sell, or disclose any personal information, CCPA compliance is straightforward:

  • No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect any personal information
  • No Data Sales: We do not sell or disclose any personal information
  • Local Data Only: All your data remains on your device
  • Complete Privacy: No external data processing or sharing

Data Breach Procedures

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any data to external servers, there is no risk of a data breach through our services. All your data remains on your device only. However, in the unlikely event of a security issue with the app itself:

  1. We will assess the scope and impact
  2. Notify users through app updates within 72 hours
  3. Provide guidance on protecting your local information
  4. Update the app to address any security concerns
